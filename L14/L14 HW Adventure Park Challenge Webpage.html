Did You Know?

Aventura Parks is home to the largest Zipline adventure park in Dubai spanning 35,000 square meters of Ghaf tree forest.

Since 2017, it has been “the place” where friends, families, students, and corporate teams can escape the hustle and bustle of city life for some exciting, safe, and unforgettable experiences!

Location

Located in Mushrif Park, Dubai, our unique attraction includes circuit-style obstacles with over 24 ziplines, an experiential learning Team Building Park specially designed for schools and organizations, and Dubai's number one outdoor escape game. Memorable once experienced with friends.
