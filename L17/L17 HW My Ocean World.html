Featured Sea Animals

Seal

Seals are semi-aquatic mammals, also known as pinnipeds. These are the only marine mammals that feed in water and breed on land. Like many aquatic animals, seals face several threats to their survival, including entanglement and commercial hunting.!

Shark

Sharks are famously credited with the ability to smell blood from the farthest distance, capable of detecting a drop of blood from up to a quarter of a mile to 3 miles away depending on the species and ocean currents.

Jellyfish

While many jellyfish stings are harmless or cause only mild irritation, some species possess highly toxic venom that can be excruciating or rapidly fatal. The danger depends on the species, the severity of the sting, and the individual's physical reaction.
