Facts About Japan

Japan is an East Asian island nation with a population of over 122 million, featuring a capital in Tokyo.

Japan is globally renowned for its unique blend of ancient tradition and futuristic innovation, featuring iconic landmarks like Mount Fuji, advanced technology (robotics, automotive), and a distinct pop culture (anime, manga, video games). Key staples include exquisite cuisine (sushi, ramen), meticulous hospitality (omotenashi), efficient transportation like the Shinkansen, and serene, historic temples

Japan is a beautiful country.
